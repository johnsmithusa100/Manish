Chatham House Debuts New Twilight Dinner Menu

Early evening menu offers special selections and value

LAUDERDALE-BY-THE-SEA, Fla. - (September 23, 2013) Chatham House, a new restaurant in the heart of Lauderdale-by-the-Sea, has introduced a new Twilight Dinner Menu.

The Twilight Dinner Menu offers three courses for a set price of $19.95 beginning with either the soup of the day or the Chatham House salad, a choice of four entrée specialties and a choice of three desserts served with coffee or tea. The menu is available nightly from 5 to 7 p.m.

The choice of entrée includes:
  • Cedar Plank Salmon with a grain-citrus mustard glaze. Jasmine rice pilaf, steamed asparagus, sundried tomato-lemon sauce-frizzled leeks
  • Sautéed Panko Crusted Chicken Breasts topped with artichokes, tomato capers in a fresh basil white sauce served with roasted garlic mashed potatoes
  • Veal Marsala, tender veal scaloppini in a Marsala wine sauce with wild mushrooms, or Veal Piccata, veal scaloppini in lemon caper butter sauce, both served with linguini aglio
  • Nona's Meatballs with Linguini, topped with a plum tomato pomodoro sauce with fresh basil
Dessert choices, which are served with coffee or tea, include:
  • Warm pistachio pound cake
  • Tiramisu
  • Fresh fruit cup

"We designed the special Twilight Dinner Menu to kick off the new season and highlight a variety of our signature dishes," said Chef Shanshank Agtey. "So many of us are on our way to the theater, a concert or another event and this menu is the perfect start to the evening."

Chatham House offers fresh seafood and more served to perfection in a New England-inspired seaside setting. The robust menu offers delicious appetizers, specialty entrees including fine fish, steak, veal, chicken and pasta, and unique desserts. A comprehensive wine list features a variety of wines by the glass and the bottle. Guests will enjoy live musical entertainment from 7:00 to 11:00 p.m. on Friday and Saturday in the expansive bar area. A private room is available for parties.

Chatham House is located at 4331 N. Ocean Drive (A1A just south of Commercial Boulevard). The restaurant is open for Monday through Saturday at 5 p.m. For reservations or more information, call 954.351.0310 or visit ChathamHouseRestaurant.com.
