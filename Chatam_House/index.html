Chatham House

Located just one block from the beach in the heart of picturesque Lauderdale-by-the-Sea, Chatham House will exceed your expectations. Known for is known for its fresh food, specialty cocktails, excellent service and a warm, inviting atmosphere, Chatham House is the perfect choice for a quiet evening on the town, a celebratory event shared with family and friends or a casual night of appetizers and specialty cocktails in the open, expansive bar area.

The restaurant serves as variety of menu specialties including fresh local fish, Angus beef and free range hormone free chicken entrees in a New England-inspired seaside setting. We invite you to experience Chatham House tonight. Reservations are just a phone call away.
